# OpsDesk

OpsDesk is a modern asset management and maintenance ticketing system. It allows organizations to track physical and IT assets, manage maintenance/repair tickets, assign work to staff, and monitor operational status across departments.

## Core Entities

- **Assets** - Physical items (devices, appliances, furniture, IT equipment, facilities) tracked by code, type, vendor, and status
- **Tickets** - Work orders for repairs, maintenance, requests, or incidents linked to assets, with priority and status tracking
- **Users** - Staff members with roles, assignable to tickets
- **Departments** - Organizational units grouping users and assets

## Key Workflows

1. Asset registration and lifecycle management
2. Ticket creation linked to one or more asset items
3. Ticket assignment to users/teams with priority and due dates
4. Status progression: New → Assigned → Doing → Waiting → Done/Cancelled
